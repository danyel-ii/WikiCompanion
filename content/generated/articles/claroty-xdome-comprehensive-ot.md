# Claroty XDome: Comprehensive OT

Claroty XDome: Comprehensive OT shows up in the upstream cybersecurity skills collection as a practical way to make sense of signals, artifacts, or system state without turning the workflow into guesswork. ## What it is

This skill covers performing vulnerability assessments in OT environments using the Claroty xDome platform for comprehensive asset discovery, risk scoring, vulnerability correlation, and remediation prioritization. It addresses passive vulnerability identification through traffic analysis, active safe querying of OT devices, integration with CVE databases and ICS-CERT advisories, and risk-based prioritization that accounts for operational impact and compensating controls. Claroty XDome: Comprehensive OT usually sits in the middle of a workflow rather than at the very start or very end. The tool tends to matter because it gives shape to noisy inputs and turns them into outputs that other parts of the investigation can use.

The source skills treat Claroty XDome: Comprehensive OT less like magic and more like a translator. ## Limits and common mistakes

A recurring mistake is to confuse tool output with final truth. Claroty XDome: Comprehensive OT is useful because it sharpens interpretation, but it still depends on context, data quality, and the analyst's framing. Another mistake is to treat it as a complete workflow by itself when the surrounding evidence and follow-up analysis usually matter just as much.
