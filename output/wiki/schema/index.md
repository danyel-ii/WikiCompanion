# Wiki Schema

This folder organizes the broader companion corpus through the same six-phase model used by the maintained wiki:

- [Recon](./recon.md)
- [Web](./web.md)
- [Exploit](./exploit.md)
- [Creds](./creds.md)
- [Post](./post.md)
- [Pivot](./pivot.md)

The maintained wiki lives at [Cybersecurity Research Wiki](../../../../MyWiki/cyber-research-wiki/wiki/index.md). Use these schema pages to move between the flatter companion dump and the curated category structure.
